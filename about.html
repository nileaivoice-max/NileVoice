About NileVoice AI

Giving Every Language a Voice

At NileVoice AI, we believe that language is more than words—it is identity, culture, history, knowledge and connection.

South Sudan is one of the most linguistically diverse nations in Africa, with dozens of languages spoken across its communities. These languages carry generations of wisdom, tradition and cultural heritage.

NileVoice AI was founded to change that by building tools that preserve, promote and empower South Sudanese languages through digital innovation.

We believe every voice deserves to be heard, understood and preserved.

Our Mission

Making communication accessible for all

NileVoice AI is dedicated to creating powerful language tools that connect communities across South Sudan and beyond. Our mission is to provide accurate, accessible and affordable translation tools while preserving the rich linguistic heritage of the country.

To achieve this, we are developing a comprehensive platform that supports the unique languages of South Sudan and helps users communicate, learn and share culture in their own voice.

90%

of our work is focused on improving access to language tools for underserved communities.

Our Vision

To create a future where every South Sudanese can communicate, learn and access information in their preferred language

We envision a future where translation technology not only connects people, but also preserves endangered languages, empowers learners and supports cultural continuity.
