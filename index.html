Because No Language Should Be Left Behind

South Sudan is home to some of Africa's richest linguistic diversity. Our languages carry our stories, cultures, traditions, identities, and generations of knowledge.

Yet many of these languages remain invisible in today's digital world.

Millions of people communicate daily in languages that are rarely supported by modern technology, making it harder to access information, connect across communities and preserve valuable cultural heritage.

NileVoice AI was founded to change that.

We are building a translation platform dedicated to South Sudanese languages—helping people understand one another, preserve their linguistic heritage and create a future where language is no longer a barrier to communication.

We build tools for translation, learning and connection so every language can thrive in the digital age.
